Empacadora El Llano is responsible for the cultivation, harvesting, handling, production, packaging, and distribution of avocados and avocado-derived products such as guacamole, avocado oil, and the sale and distribution of seeds.

Located at: Carretera Desviación a Corona km 0.5, Colonia Peribán, Michoacán, México.

El Llano Packaging has its own logistics team who facilitates delivery at the border complying with all the permissions that the government expects on their end.
